JSNinja: Chapter 9

  • windows.location.href --> page's url
  • location.href/protocol/host/hostname/port/pathname/search/hash/origin/reload/assign/replace/toString... several different methods!
  • window.history.go(n) --> go forward or backwards a few a pages (1: go forward (window.history.forward()), -1: go backwards (window.history.back), 0: reloads current page
  • window.open(), (and several other methods),
  • window.scree/n.availWidth, window.screen.availHeight (these can come in handy)
  • document.write()!
  • Cookies are okay, but session and API are much better
  • document.cookie = "city" = "Emag City"
  • cookies keep growing, you can convert them to arrays with a few split()s and some work
  • to remove a cookie, set expiration date to some time in the past
